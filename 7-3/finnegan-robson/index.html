About Me- Finn

Cricket

Cricket is a sport that is played all over the world. I really like it and it is a big part of my life. The object of the game is to get everyone on one team out before they score many points and then you have to score more points than them before you get all out. It's also really fun to watch. It's easy to get because it's so popular and played on both hemispheres so, no matter what time of year it is, there's cricket on.

Chess

I enjoy playing chess with my family and friends. My uncle teaches chess at a school for his job and he has taught me a lot of different hints and tricks in the game. My favourite opening is the French defence or the Queen's gambit. I also learned the Hippopotamus strategy which is tactical, but it's not the best.
