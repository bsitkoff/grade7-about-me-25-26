My ethnicity

Source:stimson.org

I am half Indian and half Chinese. I also identify as American.

Indian

My mother is Sindhi, which is a type of Indian. My grandparents were born in what is now Pakistan, during partition. After they came to America, my Grandfather opened a saree business, and had my mom!

Chinese

I am also Chinese. I like Chinese food, and I especially love dim sum! I have gone to hot pot once, and I really loved it! While it is true that you are paying to make your own food, it is about the experience, which is the best!
