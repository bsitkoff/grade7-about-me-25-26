Rock climbing

Rock climbing is my main sport. I have been climbing for 6-7 years (actually!). I am currently on the Comp team at Boston Bouldering Project. Climbing is such a great sport for me, and I love bouldering!

Ninja Warrior

Ninja Warrior is one of those sports where if you do it, you have to be really good. Unfortunately for me, most of the kids that I compete against have been doing it for 5 years, while I have only done it for 2. At my last competition, I got 25th out of 30. Still, it is really fun!

Taekwondo

I have been doing taekwondo for 4 years, and it has been a wild ride. I am a 1st degree black belt. I have wanted to quit 3 times, but never committed. At some point, it just became part of my identity.
