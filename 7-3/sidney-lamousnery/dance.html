Dance is a huge part of my life. I've been a dancer since I was two years old.

I currently dance at a cultural arts center, where we learn techniques from many genres of dance.

My favorite style of dance is jazz and ballet. I recently participarted in a summer intensive for the prestigious Boston Ballet School!

This year I got promoted to a higher level with girls from high school. I love all my dance friends, they're like my sister. We are all so close!

Although, dance is very tiresome and lots of hard work and persistence, it is still my favorite part of the days. I will alway love to dance no matter what!
